⚙️ Mechanics

Centre of Mass (COM)

🔍 Kya hai?
Ek imaginary point jahan poore system ka mass concentrated maan sakte hain.

🧠 Kyun padhein?
Multiple particles ko ek hi point ki tarah treat kar sakte hain, motion simplify hota hai.

🛠️ Real-life use:
Robots, rockets, gymnastics mein COM stability zaroori hai.

🤯 Agar nahi hota toh?
System ka motion samajhna mushkil ho jaata.

📜 Kisne banaya?
Archimedes → Galileo → Newton → Euler

💡 Real-life seekh:
Balance life mein bhi zaroori hai jaise motion mein.

Sum of Internal Forces = 0

🔍 Kya hai?
System ke andar jitne bhi particles ek doosre pe force lagate hain, unka net sum zero hota hai.

🧠 Kyun padhein?
COM aur momentum conservation samajhne ke liye fundamental hai.

🛠️ Real-life use:
Rocket explosions, gymnast ke jump mein system analysis.

🤯 Agar nahi hota toh?
System analysis impossible ho jaata, physics break ho jaata.

📜 Kisne samjhaya?
Newton's Third Law ke basis par concept bana.

💡 Real-life seekh:
Internal blame-shame system ka progress nahi badhata, teamwork chahiye.

Conservation of Momentum

🔍 Kya hai?
Closed system mein total momentum constant rehta hai agar external force na ho.

🧠 Kyun padhein?
Collision, explosion, COM ke motion ka base concept hai.

🛠️ Real-life use:
Rocket propulsion, car crash analysis, billiards.

🤯 Agar nahi hota toh?
Motion prediction impossible hoti. Mechanics ka base toot jaata.

📜 Kisne diya?
Newton + modern derivation se aaya.

💡 Seekh:
Jo effort daaloge woh result milega — nothing goes waste.

Newton's Laws of Motion

🔍 Kya hai?
Tin laws jo motion aur force ka relation define karte hain.

🧠 Kyun padhein?
Har motion problem ka base — force ke bina motion samajhna mushkil.

🛠️ Real-life use:
Seat belt design, elevator physics, walking, vehicles.

🤯 Agar nahi hote?
Physics vague hoti — motion ke liye koi law hi nahi hota.

📜 Kisne diya?
Sir Isaac Newton (1687)

💡 Seekh:
Force ke bina kuch move nahi karta — life mein bhi push chahiye hota hai.

Torque

🔍 Kya hai?
Rotational motion ke liye force ka effect — twisting force.

🧠 Kyun padhein?
Rotation, equilibrium, levers, wheels sab ke analysis ke liye important.

🛠️ Real-life use:
Steering wheel, wrench, ceiling fan motor.

🤯 Agar nahi hota?
Rotation ke problems samajhna mushkil ho jaata.

📜 Kisne samjhaya?
Euler + rotational mechanics ke pioneer scientists.

💡 Seekh:
Right direction me force lagao, result milega — warna ghoomte rahoge.
