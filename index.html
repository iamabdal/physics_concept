Centre of Mass (COM)

🔍 Kya hai?

Ek imaginary point jahan poore system ka mass concentrated maan sakte hain. Motion analysis simplified ho jata hai.

🧠 Kyun padhein?

Multiple particles/system ko single particle ki tarah treat karne ke liye. Complex motion simplify hota hai.

🛠️ Real-life use

Cars, robots, gymnastics, rockets — sab mein COM ki stability important hoti hai. Engineering design ka base.

🤯 Agar yeh concept nahi hota toh?

Motion analysis bohot complex ho jata. Internal motion samajhna impossible hota. Engineering design unsafe ho jaate.

📜 Kisne develop kiya?

Archimedes → Galileo → Newton → Euler. Step by step develop hua over centuries.

💡 Real-life se kya seekh milti hai?

Balance zaroori hai — jaise COM se body stable hoti hai, waise life mein bhi balance hona chahiye.

🛠️ Tum kya bana sakte ho?

Self-balancing robot, COM visualizer app, sports tracker tool, or a physics quiz site.

📚 JEE use

COM questions aate hain motion, explosion, collision, and pulley systems mein. Highly tested area.
